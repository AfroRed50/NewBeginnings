New Beginnings has four convenient locations serving Louisville Metro and the surrounding counties. Contact our friendly support staff weekdays between 9:00 AM and 4:30 PM to schedule an appointment.

New Beginnings is a private mental health clinic specializing in programs for adult DUI offenders, drug and alcohol issues and domestic violence offender treatment (BIP). New Beginnings is licensed by the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
